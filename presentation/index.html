The RodSinski Electric Go Kart Project
The 16850 lithium ion cell
Powering everyday technology...
...and used by Tesla in their electric cars
Yet discarded at the slightest issue, or made to die so you will buy again
There's energy in our waste!
We've been inspired by the booming online community of makers who harvest this resource to power their DIY electric vehicles
Such as Jehu Garcia, the inspiration for this project, who revamped a derelict VW samba into a fully functional electric vehicle
Also using the same technology to build solar power packs, bringing relief to many Puerto-Ricans who lost their homes last year
We want to do the same - combining Greg's love for racing and mechanics, and Ibraheem's passion for electrical engineering...
...to build an electric go kart
To power it, we will harvest and recondition cells from broken laptop batteries
Perfectly good cells can be found in broken batteries, often only the control board or a few cells die
We've begun collecting materials, and are starting to recondition old batteries
We'll construct a wooden frame and repurpose old parts from Greg's track to build it
